What made us decide on our project?

One of the biggest issues with recipes on the internet is how much filler content goes into the webpage. Things like author's personal insights, huge ads, and so many pictures, means that the actual content you're looking for (the recipe!), just gets pushed to the bottom of the page. Our goal in this project was to make the process of finding recipes much easier, so you can focus on the most important thing: cooking dinner!

Future work...

One of the things we missed out on working in this project was a web-scraping tool that would allow us to fetch information from the URL given by the Edamam API and format it on our site. This is definitely something to work on in the future.
